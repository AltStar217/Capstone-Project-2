Features

Secure Identity Handshake

A dynamic Verification Code system that unlocks only when the hero and user are in physical proximity. This ensures the person arriving is the genuine hero assigned to your call, preventing imposters.

Instant Signal Dispatch

A one-tap emergency button that uses Precision Geolocation to broadcast your distress signal to the nearest available hero. It eliminates the need for typing during a crisis.

Tactical Hero Profiles

Displays a hero’s Verified Skillset, past mission success rate, and real-time status. Users see exactly who is responding and what specific "powers" or tools they are bringing to the scene.
